We are Vesna.

We specialize in 100% non-toxic, ecofriendly house cleaning that is safe for you, your family, and our employees.

Based out of Santa Monica, Calif., vesna was founded as a small initiative for local families in our beautiful beach community looking for a better way to care for their home.

We are licensed, bonded, and fully insured. and, unlike most other cleaning companies, we take the mystery out of estimating the pricing of your home.
